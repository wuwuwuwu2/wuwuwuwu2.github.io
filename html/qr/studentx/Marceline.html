ENG: For a long time I thought my queer story didn't exist, but I've realized that's only because my story has been stagnated by society. Although I've known I was queer since I was 9 I had to hide that fact for another 10, having only a small handful of people who knew the truth about me, the first person I told, who in return told me they wouldn't want to be friends with "people like that", and another group who told me the whole community should be exterminated, "but I'm the cool one".

Luckily I was able to discover the safe space at the Pride TM Center (home of Identity.Education) where I could truly express my identity and found a community of supportive people, and many new friends.
